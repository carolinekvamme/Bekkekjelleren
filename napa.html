Napa Valley is a wine region in Napa County in the northern part of California, and houses over 400 wineries. Not only is the region famous for it's magnificent wines, it also is the home to many rare species. The county also has several Michelin-starred restaurants. Because of the county's location, the region has experienced some threathening fires. Especially the past few years have been threathened by fires.

California is the biggest wine producing state in the US, making over 90 percent of the total wine production in the states. In fact, California produces such a large amount of wine that if the state was a country it would be the fourth leading wine-producing country in the world. The three leading countries are France, Italy and Spain. In October 2018, just prior to establishing Bekkekjeller’n, we spent a week in Napa Valley, USA, visiting some of our favorite wineries.

The view when tasting is magnificent, and led us to bring home a nice selection of their wines.
